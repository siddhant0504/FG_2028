Why ETFs, not cash savings
Gold & Silver ETFs track physical prices exactly. Saving ₹15K cash while gold rises 8% means falling behind. With Gold ETF, you always stay in sync — redeem at month 22 and buy at that day's price.
The ₹62K equity bonus
₹22K/month in equity SIPs at 14% CAGR generates ~₹62K in gains over 22 months — the difference between 28 months and 22. Compounding is the core engine of this plan.
Silver timing is smart now

Should You Add Crypto & Alternatives?
Bitcoin / Ethereum
CoinDCX · WazirX · Mudrex Auto-Invest
CONDITIONAL YES
✓ Pros: Highest return potential of any asset class. Low correlation to Indian equities. Mudrex supports crypto SIP from ₹100/day. BTC 300%+ in last bull run.
✗ Cons: 30% flat tax on ALL crypto gains — no loss offset, no indexation, TDS 1% on every sell. 50–60% drawdowns common. Could derail 22-month goal if overallocated.
My call: Add ₹3,000–5,000/month ONLY if emergency fund already exists. Never replace existing SIPs. BTC 70% + ETH 30%. Use Mudrex for auto-invest SIP. Treat crypto gains as a bonus — not part of the gold/silver plan.
Sovereign Gold Bonds (SGB)
RBI-backed · NSE/BSE secondary market
YES — IF AVAILABLE
✓ Pros: 2.5% p.a. extra interest on gold price (tax-free at 8yr maturity). Zero storage risk, zero GST. Tax-free LTCG if held to maturity.
✗ Cons: RBI paused new SGB series since 2024. Buy on NSE/BSE secondary market via demat. 8-year lock-in — you won't complete it for this goal.
My call: Buy SGBs on NSE/BSE secondary market via Zerodha/Upstox if available at <3% premium. Gives gold tracking + 2.5% pa interest. Replace up to 50% of Gold ETF allocation with SGBs.
US Stocks / Nasdaq 100
Motilal Oswal Nasdaq 100 FOF · LRS route
SKIP FOR NOW
✓ Pros: USD exposure, S&P 500 compounding, natural INR hedge.
✗ Cons: Parag Parikh Flexi Cap already holds Alphabet, Meta, Microsoft — you're covered. FOF double expense ratio. LRS compliance above ₹7L is complex.
My call: Skip entirely. Your international diversification is already handled via Parag Parikh. Adding more is redundant with zero incremental benefit in a 22-month window.
NPS — National Pension System
Tier 1 · Tax saving instrument · Separate goal
CONSIDER SEPARATELY
✓ Pros: ₹50K extra deduction under 80CCD(1B) — saves ₹15K–17.5K in tax if 30% bracket. Equity NPS historically ~12–14% CAGR. Use the tax saving to top up this plan.
✗ Cons: Locked till age 60. 40% must be annuitised on exit. Not relevant for 22-month gold/silver goal.
My call: Invest ₹50K/year in NPS Tier 1 separately — purely for tax saving. Route the ₹15K saved into this gold/silver plan as a bonus top-up. Bonus strategy, not replacement.
P2P Lending / REITs
Faircent · LenDenClub / Embassy REIT · Mindspace
SKIP BOTH
P2P: 10–12% returns but real NPA risk, poor liquidity, evolving RBI oversight. Not worth it when HDFC Mid-Cap gives 15–18% with full daily liquidity.
